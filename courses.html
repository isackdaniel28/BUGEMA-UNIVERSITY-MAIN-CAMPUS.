COURSES WE OFFER

We offer a wide range of courses in various fields of study.
Our courses are designed to provide students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in their chosen careers.

SCHOOL OF EDUCATION

Our education courses are designed to prepare students for careers in teaching and education administration. We offer courses in early childhood education, primary education, secondary education, and special education.

SCHOOL OF BUSINESS

Our business courses are designed to prepare students for careers in business and management. We offer courses in accounting, finance, marketing, human resource management, and entrepreneurship.

SCHOOL OF HEALTH AND ALLIED SCIENCE

Our health sciences courses are designed to prepare students for careers in healthcare and medical research. We offer courses in nursing, public health, medical laboratory technology, and pharmacy.

SCHOOL OF SCIENCE AND INFORMATION TECHNOLOGY

Our information technology courses are designed to prepare students for careers in IT and computer science. We offer courses in software development, network administration, cybersecurity, and data science.

SCHOOL OF AGRICULTURE AND ENVIRONMENTAL SCIENCES

The School of Agriculture and Environmental Sciences at Bugema University is dedicated to fostering sustainable agricultural development and environmental stewardship. Through innovative research, hands-on practical training, and community engagement, we equip students with the vital skills needed to address global food security challenges and manage natural resources responsibly in East Africa and beyond.

Arts and Humanities

Our arts and humanities courses are designed to prepare students for careers in the creative industries. We offer courses in literature, history, philosophy, music, and visual arts.

Science and Engineering

Our science and engineering courses are designed to prepare students for careers in scientific research and engineering. We offer courses in physics, chemistry, biology, civil engineering, mechanical engineering, and electrical engineering.

Law

Our law courses are designed to prepare students for careers in the legal profession. We offer courses in criminal law, civil law, international law, and human rights law.

SCHOOL OF SOCIAL SCIENCE

Our social sciences courses are designed to prepare students for careers in social research and policy analysis. We offer courses in sociology, psychology, political science, and economics.

Environmental Studies

Our environmental studies courses are designed to prepare students for careers in environmental management and conservation. We offer courses in environmental science, ecology, sustainable development, and climate change.

Media and Communication

Our media and communication courses are designed to prepare students for careers in journalism, public relations, and media production. We offer courses in mass communication, digital media, advertising, and media ethics.

Public Administration

Our public administration courses are designed to prepare students for careers in government and public service. We offer courses in public policy, governance, and administrative law.

SCHOOL OF THEOLOGY AND RELIGIOUS STUDIES

The School of Theology and Religious Studies at Bugema University stands at the heart of our mission, preparing dedicated spiritual leaders, pastors, and theologians. Grounded in biblical truth and a commitment to global service, our programs combine deep scriptural scholarship, ministerial ethics, and practical field evangelism to equip students for transformative ministry across East Africa and the global church.
